In a country as vast and diverse as India, there are numerous underrated destinations that often escape the limelight but possess the power to astound and mesmerise. While popular tourist destinations like the Taj Mahal in Agra and Goa beaches receive significant attention, there are several hidden gems scattered across the country that often go unnoticed. Beyond the well-trodden paths and popular tourist spots, lies a tapestry of lesser-known destinations in India that hold secrets waiting to be discovered. These underrated gems have the power to awaken your senses and ignite your wanderlust like never before. In this blog, we unveil a carefully curated list of India's best-kept secrets – enchanting places that will not only captivate your imagination but also leave you breathless with their beauty. 1. Auli, Uttarakhand: A Winter Wonderland Nestled in the Garhwal Himalayas, Auli is a serene hill station known for its picturesque vistas and incredible skiing opportunities. Covered in a blanket of snow during winters, Auli offers breathtaking views of the towering Nanda Devi and other surrounding peaks. It's an ideal destination for adventure enthusiasts and nature lovers alike. How to get there? By Air: The nearest airport is Jolly Grant Airport in Dehradun, approximately 280 km from Auli. From there, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach Auli. By Road: Auli is well-connected by road. You can drive from major cities like Dehradun, Rishikesh, or Haridwar, or take a bus to Joshimath, followed by a ropeway or taxi ride to Auli. 2. Doodhpathri, Kashmir: A Hidden Meadow Kashmir is renowned for its scenic beauty, but amidst its popular destinations lies the untouched paradise of Doodhpathri. Often referred to as the "Valley of Milk," this hidden meadow captivates visitors with its lush green meadows, gurgling streams, and snow-capped mountains. It's a haven for those seeking solitude and a close connection with nature. How to get there? By Air: Srinagar International Airport is the closest airport to Doodhpathri, located approximately 44 km away. From the airport, you can hire a taxi to reach Doodhpathri. By Road: Doodhpathri is well-connected by road. You can hire a taxi or take a bus from Srinagar to reach this serene destination. 3. Bangaram Island, Lakshadweep: A Tropical Paradise Escape to the pristine Bangaram Island in Lakshadweep, an unexplored gem in the Arabian Sea. With its turquoise waters, pristine white sandy beaches, and vibrant coral reefs, this secluded island offers a truly idyllic getaway. Immerse yourself in the tranquillity, indulge in water sports, and marvel at the untouched marine life. How to get there? By Air: Agatti Airport is the only airport in Lakshadweep that operates regular flights. From Agatti, you can take a boat or a helicopter to reach Bangaram Island. By Sea: Ships and boats are available from Kochi (Cochin) in Kerala to reach Bangaram Island. The journey may take approximately 20-24 hours. 4. Mawlynnong, Meghalaya: The Cleanest Village in Asia Located in the abode of clouds, Meghalaya, Mawlynnong holds the distinction of being the cleanest village in Asia. This charming village enthralls visitors with its well-manicured gardens, bamboo walkways, and the famous living root bridges. Explore the rich cultural heritage, relish local delicacies, and witness the warm hospitality of the Khasi people. How to get there? By Air: Shillong Airport, also known as Umroi Airport, is the nearest airport to Mawlynnong, located around 90 km away. From the airport, you can hire a taxi to reach Mawlynnong. By Road: Mawlynnong is well-connected by road. You can take a taxi or bus from Shillong or Guwahati to reach this charming village. 5. Velas, Maharashtra: A Turtle Haven Step into the coastal village of Velas, Maharashtra, and embark on an extraordinary journey of conservation and natural beauty. Velas is renowned for its efforts in preserving Olive Ridley Sea Turtles, and visitors can witness the nesting and hatching of these magnificent creatures. The tranquil beaches and the quaint village atmosphere add to the charm of this hidden gem. How to get there? By Air: The nearest airport is Mumbai's Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport. From there, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach Velas, which is approximately 225 km away. By Road: Velas is accessible by road. You can drive or take a bus from Mumbai or Pune to reach this turtle haven. 6. Coorg, Karnataka: The Scotland of India Nestled amidst the verdant hills of Karnataka, Coorg is a captivating destination often referred to as the "Scotland of India." This picturesque hill station offers lush coffee plantations, cascading waterfalls, and misty mountains. Immerse yourself in the aroma of coffee, indulge in thrilling treks, and witness the rich cultural heritage of the Kodava people. How to get there? By Air: The nearest airport is Mangalore International Airport, located approximately 160 km from Coorg. From the airport, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach Coorg. By Road: Coorg is well-connected by road. You can drive or take a bus from major cities like Bengaluru, Mysore, or Mangalore to reach this picturesque hill station. 7. Sandakphu, Darjeeling: A Trekker's Paradise For adventure seekers and nature enthusiasts, Sandakphu in Darjeeling is a must-visit destination. It's the highest peak in West Bengal and offers awe-inspiring panoramic views of the Himalayas, including the majestic Kanchenjunga. The trek to Sandakphu takes you through rhododendron forests, charming villages, and offers an experience that will stay etched in your memory forever. How to get there? By Air: Bagdogra Airport near Siliguri is the closest airport to Sandakphu. From there, you can hire a taxi or take a shared cab to reach Darjeeling. From Darjeeling, you can embark on the thrilling trek to Sandakphu. By Road: Sandakphu is accessible by road. You can reach Darjeeling by bus or taxi and then start your trek towards Sandakphu, which takes approximately 4-5 days. From the snow-capped peaks of Auli to the pristine beaches of Bangaram Island, each destination offers a unique experience that will leave you in awe. So, step off the beaten path, explore these hidden gems, and let their unparalleled charm blow your mind.
